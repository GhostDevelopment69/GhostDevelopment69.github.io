iPhone 13 Pro Max

The iPhone 13 Pro Max from Apple© provides a big upgrade from the previous generation of iPhones. The phone includes a whole new iOS release dubbed iOS 15 and a wide array of other useful upgrades. These upgrades include a whole new camera processor, 128GB standard/base storage space and for the first time, Apple has finally upgraded the display of the iPhone. The upgraded display, dubbed "ProMotion", comes from the iPad Pro and was made specifically for this iPhone to deliver a smooth and beautiful 120Hz display. Other features of the phone are below:

  • New A15 Bionic Processor
  • ProRes Video Output for Movie Makers
  • Cinematic Camera mode for Studio Grade Film Making
  • Introduction of 5G support
  • Storage options up to 1TB
